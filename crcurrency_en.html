Costa Rica Currency

Discover the best choice to sell and buy dollars in Costa Rica

Since 2010 Costa Rica Currency has been the reliable source to know your best option to buy and sell dollars in Costa Rica.

Costa Rica Central Bank, similar to United States' Federal Reserve, controls who can sell and buy dollars. Any authorized financial institution needs approval before changing the price in which they buy and sell.

Our app's data source is Costa Rica Central Bank. You can check as often as you want to check updates in prices. Also if you enable the app's background capabilities iOS learns when you use the app and fetches for new data before you start the app.

Version 2 offers new capabilities:

  • Universal app for iPhone and iPad.
  • Easy to use calculator
  • Works offline with last succesful data fetched
  • Shows historic data
  • Main screen can be customize to show prefered banks
  • Today extension
  • Main screen data can be exported to mail, whatsapp, excel, etc.
  • Nice graphic of historic data with export feature
  • Extra bank and financial institution information such as website, facebook and waze search
  • Customizable options like bank name, color scheme and main screen style
